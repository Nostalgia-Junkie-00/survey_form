Nen Type Quiz Explained

A Nen type quiz or test is a set of questions to determine your Mind Force category. Nen is a technique to manipulate your life energy or Aura. It is a power system you in the Hunter X Hunter series. And its users are called “Nen Users” as opposed to Non-users. The test identifies which of the six Sense types matches your personality.

1.) Let’s start by challenging your Ten skills. Are you ready to take the Hunter test?

2.) A person who killed your parents is in front of you. But your master says you should not hurt him. What do you do?

3.) What comes first? (Answer as a potential Hunter).

4.) Who is your favorite Hunter X Hunter character?

5.) Would you leave a loved one behind to achieve the highest potential of your Nen?

6.) Describe why you like Hunter X Hunter.
